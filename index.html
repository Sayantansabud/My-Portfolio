About

I'm a Final-year CSE student at KIIT skilled in Data Analysis, web development, AI & ML, and UI designs. Built data-driven apps using Python and TensorFlow. Passionate about tech, design, and building impactful solutions.

My academic journey began at Ramakrishna Mission Vidyapith, Purulia, where I developed a strong foundation in discipline and academics. Scoring above 90% in both secondary and higher secondary, I cultivated a deep interest in science and technology. This passion led me to pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering at KIIT, where I’m exploring artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software development.

Along the way, I’ve applied my knowledge through impactful projects—like an AI medical chatbot, a disease prediction app, and data analytics dashboards. I’ve also embraced my creative side through design internships, contributing to brand identities and campaigns. Today, I work at the crossroads of design, data, and development—driven by curiosity and the desire to build meaningful technology.
